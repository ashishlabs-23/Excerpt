# Site Vision: Excerpt

Excerpt is a production-grade platform for converting long-form videos into viral short clips using AI.

## 1. Core Objectives
- Seamless video upload and validation.
- Fast, AI-driven clip detection and transcription.
- Professional video cutting and enhancement (9:16 crop, captions).
- Secure, high-performance delivery via signed URLs.
